# VoiceClone — release feed

This repo is the update feed for **VoiceClone**, the offline voice cloning studio
for Windows (with a Mac beta). Installed copies check this repo to learn when a
new version is out. There are no downloads here.

**Get VoiceClone:** https://designesh.gumroad.com/l/voiceclone

Your purchase includes every update. When a new version appears here, open your
Gumroad library and download it fresh, free.

What VoiceClone does:

- Clones a voice from 1 to 3 minutes of talking, 100% on your own computer
- Checks every word it speaks with a speech recognizer, best take wins
- Lets you fix one bad word in place instead of regenerating everything
- Trains a true copy of a voice on your own GPU or CPU with one click
- Exports WAV, MP3, and subtitles from the real spoken timings

receipt email.
